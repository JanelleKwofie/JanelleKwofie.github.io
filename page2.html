      Janelle Kwofie is a Freshman Computer Science and Weber Honors College student at San Diego State University. Currently, she hopes to pursue a career in Artificial Intelligence Research specifically its intersection with sociology. Since January she has been a fellow in the Colorstack sprout fellowship where she is learning skills to sharpen her coding abilities. She hopes to obtain her degree in 3 years and go from there to a grad school program. Above all Janelle wants to contribute to making the Computer Science community accessible to everyone.
